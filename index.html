Pudding Fest

An annual event by PT Mexico which will showcase some of the latest technologies from various well-known companies in Indonesia. This event will be held online and consists of several series of events for each company that will be presenting. For event participants who wish to take part in seeing this latest technology, they are required to register on the PuddingFest website.
